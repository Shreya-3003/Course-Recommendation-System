Science Stream

The Science stream delves into subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics, offering a pathway for those interested in pursuing careers in engineering, medicine, research, and other scientific fields.The main advantage of taking science stream is that it keeps your options open.

Commerce Stream

The Commerce stream focuses on subjects like Economics, Accountancy, Business Studies, and Mathematics. It's suitable for students aiming for careers in finance, business management, commerce, and related sectors.Commerce is the second most popular career option after science.

Arts Stream

The Arts or Humanities stream encompasses subjects like Literature, History, Political Science, Sociology, and Psychology. This stream caters to students with a passion for creative arts, social sciences, and careers in fields such as journalism, literature, law, and more.
